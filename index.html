Heart It Out

Heart It Out is a mental health and well-being initiative that strives to provide accessible Mental Health to 1.5 billion people by 2030. We wish to create authentic spaces for people to explore and be themselves. Our therapeutic services, products, workshops and events are thoughtfully curated to highlight the importance of self-care and tackle mental health in urban settings. Our team of experienced Psychologists in Bangalore, cater to a variety of mental health needs including clinical therapy for those suffering from Anxiety, Depression, Mood & Eating Disorders, Grief, Sexuality & Trauma and Psychotic Disorders.

What We Can Help With

Depression

Depression is a combination of sadness, low moods, and the loss of interest in joyful activities. We help you revive your belief in yourself and regain a more holistic outlook on your life.

Anxiety

Anxiety is a persistent sense of worry, dread, and panic about the past, present, or future. We focus on helping you in building healthy coping mechanisms to manage the symptoms of anxiety

Relationships

Relationships can be difficult to navigate. We aim to help each couple find their unique voice and translate it into a relationship that is compassionate, accepting, and nurturing to both individuals.

Family

Every family has its own quirks and complexities. Family Therapy helps bridge the gap between members of a family and teaches them healthy communication patterns and boundaries.

Work Life

Work related stress is one of the most common mental health complaints in India. In our therapy sessions, we focus on helping you achieve your ideal work-life balance and develop passions outside of work.

Better Life

We are all guilty of adding an impossible list of lifestyle goals to our yearly resolutions. We are all guilty of abandoning them too in the first two months. Help us help you plan your life better!

Bipolar Disorder

Bipolar disorder is categorized by alternating phases of high and low moods. Living with bipolar disorder can be distressing and confusing. We work towards helping you find your balance and live your life to the fullest.

Career Guidance

Finding the right career is about finding the balance between your interests and capabilities. We help you identify your academic and vocational strengths and choose the perfect career for yourself
